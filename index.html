Music Event

Get ready for three unforgettable nights of music, rhythm, and pure entertainment! The Music Extravaganza 2024 brings together the most exciting performers of the year, each showcasing their unique talents on separate days. Experience the magic of live performances and immerse yourself in an electrifying atmosphere. Don't miss this chance to see your favorite artists live on stage!

September 20, 2024

Harry styles

The chart-topping sensation Harry Styles kicks off the event with his iconic blend of pop, rock, and charm. Known for his energetic stage presence and powerful vocals, Harry will be performing hits from his latest album along with fan-favorites like "Watermelon Sugar" and "Adore You." This is a night of mesmerizing music you won’t want to miss!
September 20, 2024

Dolly Simmonds

Country music icon Dolly Simmonds takes the stage for an evening of timeless classics and heartfelt storytelling. With hits like "Jolene" and "9 to 5," Dolly’s signature voice and charisma will captivate the audience, bringing the spirit of Nashville to life. Her performance promises to be an unforgettable blend of tradition, talent, and pure joy.
September 20, 2024

Jake Baldock

Closing out the event is the Grammy-winning superstar Ed Sheeran, delivering a soulful and emotional performance that spans acoustic ballads to high-energy pop. With songs like "Shape of You" and "Perfect," Ed’s unparalleled ability to connect with the audience will leave you mesmerized. Prepare to be swept away by his heartfelt lyrics and acoustic mastery.
